The History of RISE

Rise Nutrition and Wellness is an extension of Rise Personal Training Studio (PT Studio). Rise PT Studio was founded in July of 2014 and started out as a private personal training facility. It was created to offer clients and personal fitness trainers an opportunity to experience a more exclusive and customer care oriented atmosphere while also providing trainers a higher quality work environment. In 6 years time, Rise PT studio was responsible for guiding hundreds of clients through customized nutrition and fitness protocols while transforming hundreds of minds and bodies at the highest level. During the pandemic, Rise CEO and staff transitioned to working for other companies in the Telehealth/telemedicine industries dealing primarily with Hormone replacement therapy, anti-aging and preventative health care. About Johnny

Jonathan Lugo, our founder was a young skinny kid who battled with body dysmorphia and low self esteem issues which led him to finding a path to bodybuilding. He was a low end personal trainer until about 23 years old when he decided to upskill and learn more and moved to Miami Florida, landing a job at Calle steel gym. There he would learn and study under some of the greatest bodybuilders and coaches in the world. He then moved back to Long Island and launched the RISE PT studio which would lead to a 10 staff facility , training and transforming hundreds of clients.He further went on to forming a bodybuilding team of 75 competitors nationwide, training full sport teams and guiding hundreds of clients to transformations utilizing Nutrition protocols and science based optimization.
